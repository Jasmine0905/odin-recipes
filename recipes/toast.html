Fluffy french toast

Description

A dish usually served for breakfast in which bread is dipped into eggs and seasonings and then fried to a golden brown.

French toast is often topped with butter, preserves, or syrup. It is often used as a method to use up French bread that is a day or two old and has started to dry out.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

    Step 1

    Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.

    Step 2

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.

    Step 3

    Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
